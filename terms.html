Frequently Asked Questions

Q: Why don't you save pictures?

A: Why would we? We're printing them to send to you! Once it's printed, it's yours, not ours.

Q: Why don't I have an account?

A: Who needs another account? You'll just forget your password and reset it through email anyway. We skip the middleman and just use your email.

Q: What's your terms of service?

A: Pay on time and we'll ship as soon as we can. Simple as that.

Q: Can I return my stickers?

A: If we messed up (like printing it upside down... wait, stickers don't have an up side), we'll fix it. If you uploaded a blurry photo of your thumb, that's on you. It's art!

Q: Where is my order?

A: It's on an adventure! Check your email for tracking info. If the carrier pigeon got lost, let us know.
